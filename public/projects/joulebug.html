01 — The Context

A relook through the lens of Climate Change

"Sustainability is everyone's business."

Sustainability is increasingly becoming a crucial focus for businesses as both policy and consumer opinion increasingly favor environmental protection. As the threat of climate change looms, more consumers are trying to make both small and large lifestyle changes in order to reduce their personal impact on the environment and are turning to brands that help this process along.

04 — Key Learnings

Moving beyond mechanics

01. Awareness

Conformity arises with lack of awareness of self. The platform must first anchor the user in their own data before social comparison begins.

02. Cognitive Load

Conversion of physical activities to virtual demands high cognitive effort, leading to higher bounce rates if the purpose isn't transparent.

03. Purpose

A platform working to create social or behavioural impact needs to move beyond game mechanics to establish a strong, shared purpose.
